🎓 WHAT YOU DISCOVERED TODAY
① OLS squares residuals.
This means extreme points are punished much more than regular ones. One outlier far from the data can have more influence than all the other points combined.

② Leverage = distance from center.
The further a point is from the mean of X, the more "leverage" it has over the slope. High leverage points are dangerous — they can pull the line in any direction.

③ This is why we check for outliers.
In real data analysis, always visualize your data first. A single unusual observation can completely change your conclusions!
⚠️ REAL WORLD WARNING
Imagine a medical study finding that a drug HELPS patients.
One extreme outlier patient could flip the result to suggest the drug HARMS people.
This is why outlier detection matters in real research!
